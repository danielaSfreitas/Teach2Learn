Advanced

Welcome to the final stage. Here, you are a Full Mentor. At this level, you will master complex structures and focus on delivering professional explanations in English.

1. Advanced Structure: Conditionals (Hypothesis)

To teach advanced concepts, you need to talk about possibilities and consequences using If clauses:

Structure Usage (Uso) Example (Exemplo)
If + Present, Will Real possibilities "If you study, you will pass."
If + Past, Would Imaginary situations "If I were you, I would practice more."

Master Tip: Use "If I were" for all persons in formal English lessons!

2. HOW TO DELIVER A FULL LESSON

Structuring your explanation

An advanced teacher uses formal transitions to guide the student through the knowledge:

  • "Let's dive into..." - (Vamos mergulhar em...)
    Ex: "Let's dive into the history of English."
  • "It is important to highlight that..." - (É importante destacar que...)
    Ex: "It is important to highlight that practice is key."
  • "To wrap up our lesson..." - (Para encerrar nossa aula...)
    Ex: "To wrap up, remember to record your voice."

Advanced Challenges

1- Complete the hypothetical sentence:

If I (have) ________ more time, I would teach every day.

2- Professional Vocabulary: What does "To wrap up" mean?
